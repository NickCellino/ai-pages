🔎 OCR Playground (Transformers.js)

Try two different in-browser OCR demos powered by Hugging Face Transformers.js.

Demo 1: Whole-image OCR

Upload any image and extract text from the full image in one shot.

Open whole-image OCR →

Demo 2: Region OCR

Draw a rectangle over a selected area and OCR just that region.

Open region OCR →

How this works

  1. The page loads a TrOCR model through @xenova/transformers.
  2. Your image is kept in-browser and sent to the model pipeline.
  3. Predicted text is shown in the output panel.

Tip First run may take a bit while model files download and cache.
